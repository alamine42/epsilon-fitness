Physical Stress

Apply u-stressor, get gainz.

Changes in: Contractile potential (strength), cellular respiration (endurance), energy pathways (stamina)...

Consistency/Health

Make it to the gym.

Manage good days/bad days, energy levels, mood ...

Injuries happen: they are not an interruption of training, they are part of it.

We really don't know why injuries happen.

Sleep

Fountain of youth.

6-8 hours of quality sleep, depending on the individual

Nutrition

Complicated, controversial, trendy.

Macro nutrients, micro nutrients, food sources, diets, volume, frequency/timing, (in)tolerances, cookies..

We really don't know the secret to nutrition.

Keep it simple, consistent and stick with what works for you.

Mental / Motivation

The X Factor

Long term motivation (months/years), short term enthusiasm (weeks/daily WODs), game time focus.

Affected by a hundred thousand million things.

Team atmosphere matters.

Programming

Setting a path.

Planning cycles, areas of focus, volume...

CFNE Programming CompTrain, with modifications.

Doesn't work without feedback!

Also, doesn't work without consistency. Or without nutrition, or motivation, or sleep, or... you get the picture.

Genetics

Cards dealt.

Antropomorphy, baseline physical abilities, rate of development, durability...

Affects your mind, your nutrition, your sleep, and your technique.

Probably has a bigger impact than you suspect, but you can't control it.

Technique

A bunch of neurons firing.

Long-term development, many practice reps, most of them frustrating.

Does not work without strength.

Matters in some movements, more than others.

Highly individualistic.

Programming

Emphasis on strength in the offseason, emphasis on skills and WODs closer to the Open.

Modified weights, modified skill work to target our weaknesses.

Snatch + Muscle Ups

January: C2B + Power Snatches (also Wallballs, T2B, Box Jumps, Thrusters, Burpees, OHS..)

Game Planning

Have a plan.

Plan involves: rep schemes, rest breaks, progressions, equipment setup..

Plan does not involve: other competitors

Build it around the weakest movement. (C2B)

Make it conservative.

Keep it simple so you don't have to think.

Have a plan B.

Warm Up

Make it hot

General -> mobility -> wod-focused

Identify the state of the union

3-2-1 mini WOD

Mind the schedule

Performing

The goal: Exhaust all energy stores with 0:00 on the clock.

Mind your personal threshold

Execute the plan for 90% of the workout

"Sell out" for the last 10%

Count down your break / Watch the clock

Zone out

Ignore the judge

Cool Down

Hydrate
